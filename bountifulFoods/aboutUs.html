Our Background

It all began when my Grandpa Pat grew up in Solvang, CA. They grew up farming, homesteading and enjoying the hard work of living off the land. They were in great health dispite the large amounts of fresh dairy, butter, and red meat they prodominetely lived off. My father tells the story of how clean farming practice creates the food that is the most healthy for us. I was blessed with a mother who loved to cook with fresh ingredients so we new the difference in taste when we could get our vegetables and fruits right out of the ground.
This is our goal today. To provide everyday families with locally sourced organic produce to supply them the finest, nutritious products they can get.
- Mark Sterling, CEO Bountiful Foods
