White Space and Clean Design

Square Space
www.squarespace.com

This website gives a great example of white space using it with it's button showing the nice bright white that draws to the eye as well as the text and logo. The rest of their site in homepage view gives a lot of white space as well so click on the link and look around!

Hick's Law

Modular Mining
www.modularmining.com

This machine website gives a great example of Hick's Law. For a business with many different aspects, this website gives great representation of all of their different machines in a very simplistic fashion so the choice isn't overwhelming. We don't have a horribly busy page to look at for all the information given.

PARC: Contrast

The Ordinary Restaurant
www.eattheordinary.com

This restaurant website gives a great example of the use of contrast. The black and white design of the text and the background gives an obvious strong contrast. Using that same color scheme throughout the entire website gives a classic feel and really puts it's colorful food pictures in the forefront, showing off what their website is really about.
