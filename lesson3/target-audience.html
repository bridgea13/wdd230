Target Audience

The target audiences woud be the business owners and the government or city leaders, either volunteer or voted in in many aspects of the city.

Persona 1: Melissa is a local business owner wanting to meet with other business owners and network as well as advertise better. She knows she can come to the city's chamber of commerce webpage to find events or businesses she can connect with.

Persona 2: Duane is a member of the community who loves to be involved. He volunteers for many leadership roles within the community and is always participating in city events and is invested in what happens in his community. He is interested in local developement and the chamber's goals and how they will impact his community in the future.

Five scenarios:

1. Bob wants to know how he can become a member of the chamber of commerce because he is unhappy with the trajectory of his community.

2. Lisa is a member of the board and wants to be sure she is up to date with any communications and log in to the member exclusive portal.

3. Henry has a small business and wants to sponsor so he can spread word of his business throughout the community as a form of advertising.

4. Coco is a business owner and is looking to expand to grow her business in another location within the City Center's district and would like to research more info.

5. Mary is a member of the community who wants to support efforts she believes help her community and would like to attend the women's conference put on by the chamber of conference.
