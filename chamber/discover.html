Every year, our city hosts the Annual Hot Air Balloon Festival. This event attracts thousands of people from all over the world. Many food vendors are on site and we have hundreds of owners attend with their fun, bright balloons.

New Mexico and it's beautiful landscape proudly boast over 500 wildflower species. Our iconic cacti and desert views are one of a kind.

Red Rock homes the famous Alli River where adventorors can go whitewater kayaking, nestled in the beautiful Red Rock Canyon.

The great outdoors here in Red Rock attract many thrill seekers and deal seekers. Our great blend of commercial and boutique shops keep even the pickiest shopper happy.

Red Rock city has been described as the biggest little city. We have so much to offer like some of our bigger city friends, but we are able to maintain a small town feel.

These beautiful red rocks that surround our city are great for hiking and rock climbing or as a great backdrop for a photo. Come experience the beauty in person.
